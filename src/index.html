    Meet Kurren

    I'm a practising GP and Lifestyle Medicine expert who uses spoken word to inspire healthier, more balanced lives. Through my unique blend of medical expertise and artistic expression, I help individuals and organisations understand and embrace the connection between physical, mental, and emotional wellbeing.

    My performances and talks combine evidence-based medical knowledge with the emotional power of spoken word, creating memorable experiences that so far, everyone loves.
